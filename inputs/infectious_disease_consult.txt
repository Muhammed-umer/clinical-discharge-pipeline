Infectious Disease Consultation

Patient clinically improved after intravenous Ceftriaxone.

Afebrile for the last 24 hours.

Urine culture positive for Escherichia coli sensitive to Nitrofurantoin.

Recommend discontinuing intravenous Ceftriaxone.

Discharge with Nitrofurantoin 100 mg twice daily for 5 days.

Increase oral fluid intake.

Follow-up after one week with urine culture review.
